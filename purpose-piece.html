Teams have roles
and need players.

You already have one — you're a piece to the puzzle. Time to play.

Every meaningful effort — a company, a movement, a family, a civilisation — requires different kinds of contribution. Not everyone does the same work. And not everyone should. Effectiveness depends on role clarity.

Without training. Without titles. Without permission — in any situation where something needs to happen, you will naturally move in a certain way.

Some people structure. Some people build. Some people care. Some people see ahead. Some people stabilise. This pattern already exists in you. It has always existed in you.

The problem isn't ability.

Most people mis-locate themselves — not because they lack ability, but because they lack language.

Modern culture teaches identity: What am I? What do I want to be? What should I become? But teams run on function: How do you move? Where do you create value? What kind of contribution stabilises systems?

Without functional language, people overextend, under-utilise themselves, collide with others, and burn out in the wrong roles. Not because they aren't capable — because they aren't located.

Correct location changes everything.

When someone occupies their natural contribution pattern, something shifts. Friction drops. Impact rises. Collaboration stabilises. Energy is conserved. Trust increases. Leadership becomes clearer.

This is not motivation. This is alignment.

What Purpose Piece does.

Purpose Piece does not tell you who to be. It identifies how you contribute, where that contribution belongs, and at what scale it is naturally expressed.

This creates a functional map of your role in human systems.

Archetype

Your natural mode of contribution. The functional shape you take when things need to get done.

Domain

The area of reality where that contribution applies. Seven domains of collective life, each with its own question it's trying to answer.

Scale

The level at which your contribution stabilises systems. Not ambition — bandwidth.

Archetype + Domain + Scale = Your Purpose Piece. This is your full coordinate — specific enough to navigate from, flexible enough to travel across jobs, projects, and life stages.
